About Java Jam

We are a group of passionate high school and college students dedicated to providing high-quality programming education to the next generation of coders.

Our team is made up of members of the National STEM Honor Society at Westlake High School and students continuing their studies in top computer science programs such as UT Austin and MIT.

With achievements in competitions like USACO and CyberPatriot, and coursework including AP Computer Science and beyond, we bring both expertise and real-world experience to every course we teach.

Our mission is to make learning programming accessible, challenging, and inspiring for all students. Through hands-on lessons, project-based learning, and a supportive environment, we help students build strong coding skills and the confidence to keep growing.

What We Believe

Accessibility

High-quality programming education should be available to every motivated student.

Challenge

Students grow most when they are encouraged to tackle meaningful and engaging problems.

Confidence

Learning to code is not just about syntax—it's about developing confidence as a problem solver.
