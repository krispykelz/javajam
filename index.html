About Us

Java Jam is a java course intended to boost younger students' interest and skill in java, giving them a jumpstart on high school coding curriculum. The classes are taught by USACO medalists and top students at Westlake High School, and the labs and course material are intuitive, engaging, and a learning opportunity for all class attendees.
